From Kitchen to Platform

GetWhisked started in Natalie Bohchalian's kitchen — a way to combine her love of baking with giving back. Every cupcake, every loaf of Choreg, every pie was baked with love and donated to support meaningful causes.

But Natalie realized one baker can only do so much. The real question became: what if every young person with a passion for baking could do the same thing?

That's how Natalie transformed GetWhisked into a platform — powered by AI to lower the barrier for any young baker who wants to turn their kitchen into a force for good.

TRANSPARENCY

How the Money Works

We believe in full transparency. Here's exactly how your bake sale proceeds make it from your kitchen to the causes you care about.

🏠
✓

You Sell Locally

All food is sold in person at local events — school bake sales, farmers markets, community gatherings. No food is shipped or sold online, keeping things simple and compliant with local cottage food rules.

💱
✓

Online Donations Welcome

Your fundraiser page also accepts direct online donations from supporters who want to contribute without buying a treat. No food changes hands — just generosity.

✅
✓

Verified Nonprofits Only

Every charity on GetWhisked is a verified 501(c)(3) nonprofit. We vet every organization so donors and bakers can trust that their money goes where it's supposed to.

📈
✓

100% Net Proceeds Donated

After covering ingredient costs, every penny of profit goes directly to your chosen cause. Your dashboard shows exactly how much was raised, spent, and donated — full transparency.
